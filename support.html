Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ).

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the contact form or by emailing us at moongamessupport@gmail.com.

How do I recover a lost account?

To recover your account, fill out the recovery form and provide the required information.

What should I do if I find a bug?

Report the bug using the bug report form. This helps us improve the game!

Why isn't the game loading?

Try checking your internet connection, updating the app, or restarting your device. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

How can I access new features?

You can check the updates section in the game or on our website to learn about the latest features and additions
