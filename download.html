Persona 5

About Game

Persona 5 is a stylish RPG by Atlus where players control Joker, a high school student who becomes the leader of the Phantom Thieves of Hearts. Set in modern Tokyo, the group uses their powers to reform corrupt individuals by stealing their "hearts" in the Metaverse, an alternate realm shaped by distorted desires. The game combines turn-based combat, dungeon exploration, and life simulation, allowing players to balance school life, friendships, and battling supernatural threats. Known for its vibrant visuals, jazz-infused soundtrack, and deep themes of rebellion and self-discovery, Persona 5 is a standout entry in the series, with its enhanced version, Persona 5 Royal, offering even more content.
